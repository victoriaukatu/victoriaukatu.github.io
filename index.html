About Me

My journey in becoming a full-stack software engineer started with a little question of "What if...." I come from a family of medical professionals and as a college student I was prepared to follow in those footsteps. Also during this time period I discovered a few online coding programs and as I was working through the tutorials I was surprised to find that I really enjoyed it. I began to ask myself, "What if I decided to pursue computer programming?" It was a choice between following the familiarity of the well-worn path and diving head-first into the adventure of my growing interest. I decided to take that leap and I am so glad that I did. After completing an immersive software development program, I am looking for more opportunities to grow in this exciting field.
